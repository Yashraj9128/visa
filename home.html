Instructions for Visa Application

  1. Fill out the visa application form completely and accurately.
  2. Attach all required documents, including a valid passport and any supporting documentation.
  3. Pay the visa application fee.
  4. Schedule an appointment at the nearest embassy or consulate for an interview.
  5. Attend the interview with all necessary documents and be prepared to answer questions about your application.
  6. Wait for a decision on your visa application. The processing time may vary.
  7. If approved, follow the instructions for visa issuance. If denied, you will be provided with reasons for the denial.
